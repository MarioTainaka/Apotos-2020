FAQs-----------------------------------------------------------------------

Q. Why do I have Endless Loading or Crashes?
A. It might happen if you use other sound mods that use CPK archieves such as the Sonic Unleashed Result Music Mod by BrianuuSonic or you may might have installed it in wrong way.(See Installation.png for reference)

Q. How do i restart the restart the pack?
A. Open SonicGMI. Click Apotos Adventure Pack v2.0 once. Then click "Restore Save File".

*Easter Egg: you can play act 1 again if you can find it. Of course it's somewhere in the hub, somewhere where you might not think to look.
